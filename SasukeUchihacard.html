Sasuke Uchiha

Sasuke Uchiha is one of the central characters in the Naruto series, created by Masashi Kishimoto

Clan and Family: Sasuke hails from the prestigious Uchiha clan, known for its powerful ninja and the Sharingan eye technique

Akatsuki

The Akatsuki is a fictional organization in the Naruto series created by Masashi Kishimoto. Comprised of rogue ninja, the Akatsuki is known for its members' strength and the pursuit of their various goals, including the capture of the Tailed Beasts (Bijuu) for world domination.

he Akatsuki was originally formed by Yahiko with the goal of bringing peace to the war-torn Amegakure (Village Hidden in the Rain).
